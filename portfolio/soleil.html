SOLEIL EVENTS

THE BRAND

Leanne was looking for a completely new look and feel for her wedding event company. The majority of her work is set in Santa Ynez Wine Country and Santa Barbara. We drew inspiration from the natural elements used in her work and created an identity that plays off the rustic charm of Soleil Events. The brand aesthetic pays homage to the hand-crafted detail put into each wedding.

THE WEB

Soleil Events website needed a complete overhaul. We decided to create a site that focused on showcasing the work Leanne does for her clients. We added a portfolio section which showcases several successful weddings. The portfolio uses large images that fill the screen and enables users to take in the intricate details of Soleil Events.
