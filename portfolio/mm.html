THE MIRACLE OF MEDITATION

THE BOOK

Jeff wanted to turn a journal from a 60-day meditation retreat into a work of art that could be shared with others. The result was this book—available in print and virtual format—a collection of 60 days of insight from the deepest part of the self. We used striking photography from Simon Hogsberg of everyday people being captured in a moment of what appears to be inner stillness.
