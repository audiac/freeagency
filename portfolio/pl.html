PHILOSOPHY IS NOT A LUXURY

THE BOOK

Jeff didn't write this book in order to tell you what to think, but to give you some things to think about. In doing this he follows the Romantic sentiment that recognizes that the value of writing is not found in the words, but in the truth they provoke in the heart and mind of the reader. We used gorgeous, stark photography from Simon Hogsberg to emphasize the depth behind a person's face.
