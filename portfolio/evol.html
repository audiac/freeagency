EVOLUTIONARY COLLECTIVE

THE BRAND

Patricia came to us to create a new identity for her brand Evolutionary Collective. The company works with people in a unique capacity; they are interested in the space between people when they come together. This informed the logo we created which uses a formation of circles overlapping and creating a new shape and new color in their connection.

THE WEB

The website for the brand was very content-heavy with podcasts, blog content, and seminars conistently rotating. We created a dynamic site that focused on providing followers with the latest information Evolutionary Collective had available. The podcast page featured black and white portrait tiles that turned color when hovered-over and showcased what that particular podcast was about. The entire site was a custom design with colored sub-branding for each brand segment.
