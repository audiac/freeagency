SMART GROWTH REAL ESTATE

THE BRAND

Chris came to us to create a new identity for his brand Smart Growth Real Estate. We wanted to create something unique for the real estate market that promoted the company ideal of living smart. To them that means treating others with respect, developing a sustainable society, and making profitable financial decisions.

THE WEB

We developed a simple Phase I website to get the brand started. Our focal point was introducing users to Smart Growth and the three areas potential customers will want to connect about: sellers, investors, and buyers. Usability and conversion were a priority as we wanted people to get the information they needed and be able to easily connect with Smart Growth about their needs.
